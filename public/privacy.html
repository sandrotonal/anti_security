privacy policy & gdpr

at securify, we take the security and privacy of your personal data extremely seriously. we ensure all personal data is processed and stored in compliance with the general data protection regulation (gdpr) and other applicable data protection regulations.

2. data processed & purpose

3. zero-knowledge codebase architecture

securify operates on a zero-knowledge scanning model. your scanned code repositories and files are never uploaded to our servers. all static code analysis, entropy calculations, and regex parsing run completely client-side in your local browser sandbox or local CLI agent environment. your IP and credentials stay in your own secure terminal.

4. data sharing & processors

we do not sell, rent, or lease your personal data. your email address and payment details are securely transferred to our payment gateway provider paddle solely to process checkout orders and subscriptions.
